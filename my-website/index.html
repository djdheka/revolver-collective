About
We Don't Follow
The Sound.
We Define It.

Revolver Collective is not just a DJ crew — it is a movement. Born in the heart of Yogyakarta, six gunslingers came together with one shared conviction: that music is the most powerful force in a room. We bring together a spectrum of sounds, from deep underground rhythms to euphoric dancefloor anthems, crafted to move people in ways they never expected. Every set is a statement. Every night is a declaration.
